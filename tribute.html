Tribute to MS Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, fondly known as "Mahi", is a former Indian cricketer and captain of the Indian national team.
He was born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Jharkhand. Also known as "Captain Cool"

Major Achievements:

  • ICC T20 World Cup Winner in 2007
  • ICC Cricket World Cup Winner in 2011
  • ICC Champions Trophy Winner in 2013
  • 6 IPL Titles with Chennai Super Kings

Records:

Dhoni holds several records, including:

  • Most Matches as Captain in T20 Internationals
  • Highest Number of Runs in T20s for India
